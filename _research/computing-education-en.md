Here I am interested in projects focused on programming education, rigorous beginner learning tracks, written exams, and hand tracing.

I am also interested in the design of materials that combine didactic accessibility with conceptual rigor.

This line is especially suitable for investigations that remain in direct dialogue with teaching practice.

## Possible lines of work

- didactic sequencing of introductory content;
- design and evaluation of exercise lists, exams, and supporting materials;
- study of recurring difficulties in learning programming;
- investigation of the balance between didactic clarity and formal rigor.
